FAQ

Quick answers to the questions I get most often.

How should I store macarons?

Refrigerate in an airtight container. Best texture after 24 hours; enjoy within ~5 days.

Do you accommodate allergies?

Macarons contain almonds + egg whites. Fillings may contain dairy. Ask about options.

Can you match a theme or color?

Yes—share your inspo pics and palette. Custom sets need extra lead time.
